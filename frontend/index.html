About the Event

An Evening of Community & Spirituality

Join us for a beautiful evening of iftar — the blessed meal that breaks the day's fast during the holy month of Ramadan. This year, we're bringing together 40-50 Muslims from across Chennai to share food, duas, and the warmth of community.

Whether you're a student, a professional, or a family — everyone is welcome to this gathering filled with love, halal food, and the spirit of Ramadan. Let us break bread (and dates!) together. 🌙
